So what better way to do that than to find out what’s going on digitally in the third sector and to share it with you. We’ve scouted out some of the best Digital Pioneers across the third sector who are utilising digital tools and approaches within their organisations to help improve the way they work and deliver their services. Our Digital Pioneers recognise the need to continuously develop their digital skills and actively seek out new ways of doing things and we wanted you to hear all about it. Check out our Digital Pioneers stories from a range of charities and voluntary groups working across Scotland below. Don’t forget to share them with your networks and get in touch if you want to tell your Digital Pioneer story to keep the inspiration flowing!
